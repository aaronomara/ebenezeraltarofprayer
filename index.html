About Us

Background

Ebenezer Altar of Prayer Ministries is established as a Bible-based ministry. It was founded in 2019. Ebenezer Altar of Prayer Ministries has steadily grown into a spiritually vibrant community. Its values, vision, mission and belief is based on Matthew 28:19-20

"Matthew 28:19 Go ye therefore, and teach all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost: 20 Teaching them to observe all things whatsoever I have commanded you: and, lo, I am with you alway, even unto the end of the world. Amen."

Values

  • Accountability
  • Integrity
  • Stewardship
  • Team work
  • Transparency

Vision

“To reached the outermost part of the world with the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ”

Mission

“To Love, encourage, teach, equip and raise a generation that will take the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ to the outermost part of the world.”

Belief

  • Baptism
  • Holy Bible
  • Trinity
  • Dedication
  • Death and resurrection
  • Healing and deliverance
  • Speaking in tongues
